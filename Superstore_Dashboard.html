📌 Key Takeaways

  • Technology is the strongest category — highest sales and highest profit, driven by Phones and Copiers.
  • Tables, Bookcases and Machines are operating at a loss — these need pricing or discount-policy review immediately.
  • East region generates the most revenue and profit; Central lags behind across most metrics.
  • Discounts above 20% consistently erode profit margin — discount strategy should be capped or targeted.
  • Consumer segment drives over half of total sales, making it the priority segment for retention efforts.
  • Sales peak every November (holiday season) — inventory and staffing should be planned around this surge.
